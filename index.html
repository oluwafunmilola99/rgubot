IT Helpdesk

The IT Helpdesk offers support for all your university needs. We support students staff and visitors with everything such as IT accounts, how to connect to the wireless service, accessing and configuring email, print, copy and scan, and the location of IT labs across our campus - and lots more.

IT Service Desk Support Portal

The IT Service Desk Support Portal allows students and staff to log and monitor any queries or requests. Part of the Portal is a self-help Solutions area (including an FAQ section) that allows a user to view support articles and documents that may help with any issues or questions.

IT Service Desk Support Portal

Teaching and Meeting Rooms Info

IT Services support the Audio Visual equipment in 192 teaching and meeting rooms with a number of digital enhancements having taken place over summer 2021 and more scheduled over the 2021/22 Academic year.

Account & Password

Your RGU username and password will let you log in to computers on campus, plus MyApps, the VLE (CampusMoodle), Sharepoint (RGyoU), Wi-Fi (eduroam), email and much more, depending on your status.

Wireless Access

Wifi is available across the university. University staff and students should use the secure and constantly free “eduroam” wireless service with The Cloud available for non-eduroam users.

Find Out More

Email

The email service is the main form of communication for all staff and students, so why not setup your account on your phone and\or tablet using our handy guides?

Find Out More

Print, Copy & Scan

RGU uses a print queue called Print@RGU, which is available university wide, allowing both staff and students to collect print jobs from Canon Multifunctional Devices (MFDs) that are scattered across the University campus.

MyApps

Access many applications including some course software, email, H drive and S drive anywhere, as if you were on campus.
